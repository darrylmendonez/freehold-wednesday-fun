FHS Clubs for Web Design Pathway Program Students


TSA is is the only student organization devoted exclusively to the needs of technology education students.
Open to students who are enrolled in, or who have completed, technology education courses,
TSA is comprised of over 190,000 elementary, middle, and high school students in 2,000 schools spanning 48 states.
TSA is supported by educators, parents, and business leaders who believe in the need for a technologically literate society.
Our members learn through exciting competitive events, leadership opportunities, and much more!
